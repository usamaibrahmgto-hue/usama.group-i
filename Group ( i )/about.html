About Us

We are Group(i)

We are Group i, the team behind this website, proudly dedicated to supporting the NRM Presidential Election 2026. As passionate members of the NRM community, our mission is to create a digital space where voters can access accurate information, stay updated, and engage with the election process from anywhere in Uganda and beyond. This platform was built to serve as a hub for news, voter education, event updates, and diaspora involvement. We believe that technology can bridge the gap between leadership and the people, and we are committed to using this space to promote participation, unity, and transparency throughout the election period.

The Journey Towards The 2026 Election!

Meet Your favorate candidate

Yoweri Museveni is the actual president of Uganda

Candidate Selection

The National Resistance Movement (NRM) will officially announce its presidential candidate. It’s an important step that shows who will represent the party in the 2026 election. The selected candidate becomes the face of NRM’s campaign.

Election Day

The official voting for all Ugandans. Citizens will go to polling stations or by this website to vote for the President. It's a national event that decides who will lead Uganda for the next term. Peaceful participation is encouraged.

National Campaign

The NRM will hold rallies, meetings, and community visits across the country. The goal is to mobilize supporters, explain the party’s goals, and encourage people to vote. It’s a time of energy, unity, and political messaging.

Our Facilities

Well-organized venues, security, media coverage, and voter support centers will be provided throughout the election process.

Voter Assistance Desks:

Voter Assistance Desks are set up to support citizens on election day. Their main purpose is to help voters verify their registration, guide them to the correct polling booths, and resolve any issues related to voter identification. These desks will also offer information on how to vote correctly and assist vulnerable groups such as the elderly, people with disabilities, and first-time voters, ensuring that the voting process is smooth and accessible for everyone.

Media & Communication Centers

Media and Communication Centers will play a vital role in keeping the public informed throughout the election process. They will handle official announcements, provide live coverage of campaign events, and share timely updates about the election. These centers will also manage public inquiries, facilitate press briefings, and coordinate with journalists to ensure accurate reporting. Additionally, they will oversee social media and online platforms to reach a wider audience and maintain transparency.

Polling Stations

Polling Stations are the designated places where Ugandans will cast their votes. Each station will provide a secure and organized environment with trained officials overseeing the voting process. Proper arrangements will be made to ensure privacy, transparency, and the safe handling of ballots. Security will be present to maintain peace and order, ensuring every citizen can exercise their right to vote in a safe and fair setting.
